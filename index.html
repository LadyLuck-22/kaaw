Kaaw Chop Suey


Ingredients

  1. Kaaw(Herring Eggs On Kelp)
  2. bacon
  3. onions
  4. brocolli
  5. carrots
  6. celery
  7. snow peas
  8. cabbage
  9. peppers
  10. whatever veg you want
  11. butter
  12. soya sauce
  13. salt
  14. pepper
  15. garlic salt
  16. A liite oil

Directions

  1. Cut Veggies up in bite size pieces
  2. Cut Kaaw in bite size pieces
  3. Cut bacon small size
  4. cut onion
  5. fry bacon in a pot
  6. take bacon out put aside
  7. fry onion in bancon grease and a little butter
  8. spinkle salt and pepper on onions
  9. now Youn can put your hard veggies , carrots celery cabbage in the pot . to soften
  10. and now you can put the rest of your veggies in to cook
  11. Veggies are all cooked .take them out and put in dish
  12. melt butter with garlic in the pot and put Kaaw
  13. keep stirring Kaaw untill the eggs get a bit white
  14. dont cook till completely white or they will taste rubbery
  15. add your veggues back in
  16. put soys sauce all over
  17. now add your crispy bacon on top
  18. serve over rice and top with sesame seeds
